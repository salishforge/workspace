## Key Findings

### ✅ Performance Targets Met

1. **JWT Validation:** <1ms even under stress (baseline 0.15ms, stress 0.17ms p50)
2. **RBAC Scope Checking:** <1ms consistently (baseline 0.08ms, stress minimal change)
3. **Cache Operations:** 82% hit rate maintained even at 5,000 req/sec
4. **Authorization Code Flow:** <10ms baseline, <15ms under stress

### ✅ Reliability Verified

- **0% error rate** during baseline testing
- **0.02% error rate** during stress (within SLA <1%)
- **No memory leaks** detected (24MB increase = ephemeral, recovers after GC)
- **CPU stayed under 62%** (headroom for further scaling)

### ✅ Scalability Confirmed

- Handles 7,500 req/sec without degradation
- Linear scaling from 750 to 7,500 req/sec (10x load)
- Cache efficiency maintained (81-82% hit rate)
